# Food Recipe App

## A Flutter application designed to provide users with a comprehensive and interactive food recipe experience. Key features include:

### __Dish Information: Detailed recipes with preparation methods.__
### __Profile: User profile management.__
### __Favorites: Save and view favorite dishes.__
### __About: Information about the app and developers.__
### __Contact Us: Reach out to us for support or inquiries.__
### __Logout: Securely log out of the app.__
### __Search Bar: Easily search for dishes and recipes.__
### Explore various dishes, save your favorites, and enjoy an organized recipe collection with this user-friendly app.
